[Albert Einstein]

Isaac Newton is considered one of the greatest physicists in history. He formulated the three laws of motion and the law of universal gravitation, which laid the foundation of classical mechanics. His work helped scientists understand how objects move and why planets revolve around the sun. Newton’s discoveries are still taught in schools and colleges today. Without his contribution, modern engineering, space science, and physics would not have developed in the way we know them now.

Speed of Light

Did you know? Light travels at a speed of 299,792,458 meters per second. Nothing in the universe can travel faster than light!

Electromagnetism

Electricity and Magnetism are two sides of the same coin. Moving electric charges create magnetic fields.

Why is Sky Blue?

The sky looks blue because the Earth's atmosphere scatters sunlight in all directions, and blue light is scattered more than other colors.
